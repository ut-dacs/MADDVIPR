First Conference: ICT OPEN 2019

Wednesday, Mar, 2019 By Raffaele.

The MADDVIPR project attended the first conference :) Raffaele presented a poster on the state of the art of DNS vulnerability and misconfiguration to ICT.OPEN 2019

ICT.OPEN is the event for ICT researchers and industry stakeholders. More than 500 scientists and researchers from different ICT Industry come to share information and meet new people. It was an amazing experience and we receive a lot of feedback on our work.
